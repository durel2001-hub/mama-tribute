TEACHER CARO(NYAMALO)

"The greatest mother who nurtured great talent and minds"

About mum

BIRTH

Carolyne atieno was born on the 12th April 1977 at Ebbiba Esiandumba sublocation south west Bunyore in Vihiga District.

She was the first born child of Mr and Mrs Naboth Oniango.

She was a sister to Ednah Naboth,Christine Ambira,Rosebella akoyi and Stanley Naboth



EDUCATION

The late carolyne joined std one at Esiandumba primary school in the year 1982--1991.

She then joined Esalwa Secondary school in 1992-1995 and layter joined Monicah Memorial Educational Training Center in the year 2010-2011 and attained her Diploma certificate.


Work

The late Carolyne was an ECD teacher at sher moi nursery school and later joined Emmanuel Junior academy where she worked shortly.



Church

At home ,Carolyne Atieno was a full Anglican church member.She was confirmed and baptized by the late Bishop Isreal Mundia

She later joined Catholic Chucrh in Naivasha



Marriage

Mrs Carolyne got married to her late husband Joseph Onyango in the year 1998 until when she was widowed in the year 2007

They were blessed with one child named Durel Odhiambo Ogalo



Sickness

Mrs Carolyne has enjoyed good health all her life until when she started ailing in 2015 april

Her condition deteriorated form 31st May when she was admitted at poly clinic naivasha, and later on rested in the company of her beloved mother Alice Naboth who has taken care of her all through at around 13.20 pm on 17th June 2018.

She died at the age of 40 years
